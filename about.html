ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Isabella Sterling, a renowned jewelry designer,
was born into a family with a long history in the jewelry business.Designers often need to interact with clients, suppliers, and other professionals. Being able to communicate effectively and professionally can help ensure smooth collaborations and customer satisfaction. Each of these skills contributes to the effective functioning of a jewelry designer, helping them not only in the design process but also in managing the various interactions and complexities inherent in the profession. While these skills can be developed and honed over time, a successful jewelry designer is typically one who has a balanced blend of artistic talent, technical proficiency, and business acumen.
